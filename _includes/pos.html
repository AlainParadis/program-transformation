Reimagined Program of Study

This program of study was created (with A.I. assistance). It's not meant for implementation. It's meant to demonstrate what is possible.

  • Six semesters. 30 courses, five per semester.
  • 25 core courses. Core share: 83.3%, under the 85% cap.
  • 5 non-core courses. Non-core share: 16.7%. This clears the 15% minimum.
  • Non-core must cover Indigenous knowledge plus at least two of: humanities, sciences, social sciences, global cultures, mathematics.
  • A work term of at least 300 hours, related to the field, in the final semester.
  • At least 15% of program hours in non-core (breadth) courses.
  • At least one freely chosen elective.
