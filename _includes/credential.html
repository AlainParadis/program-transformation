Credential

While the current three-year program is successful, this initiative requires that we explore alternatives to address current and evolving external and internal factors.

Decision scope:
Should the Algonquin College Graphic Design program maintain an Advanced Diploma, pursue 3-year applied degree, or pursue an alternative (e.g., pathways/articulations).

For what it's worth, Canada’s federal Job Bank states that graphic designers typically need either a university degree in visual arts with a specialization in graphic design or completion of a college diploma in graphic arts/graphic design. That could simply be because it's the most common credential in the field. A degree could be more attractive and beneficial nonetheless.

Decision Criteria

  • Postsecondary Education Quality Assessment Board (PEQAB) feasibility (faculty credentials, scholarship, breadth).
  • Student demand and labour-market outcomes.
  • Financial sustainability.
  • Alignment with Algonquin’s Program Quality Assurance Framework and strategic plan.
  • Impact on faculty workload, culture, and equity.
  • Is it possible to know enrolment numbers for degree programs in the province?
  • Should we survey current Graphic Design students for interest in a degree option?

Evidence Types

Quantitative
  • Enrolment trends
  • Application yield
  • Retention
  • Graduation rates
  • Employment outcomes
  • Salary surveys
  • Transfer rates
Qualitative:
  • Focus groups
  • Interviews
  • Open‑ended survey responses
  • PAC minutes
  • Faculty consultations.
External
  • PEQAB manuals and benchmarks
  • Other colleges’ review guidelines
  • Sector studies on new program approvals
Internal process evidence
  • Algonquin’s program quality assurance reviews and prior program change cases.

Faculty Credentials

The minimum credential for faculty teaching in a 3-year applied degree program is the terminal credential, which is a Master's degree. Fifty percent of core courses are taught by Master's holders. There's a minimum of five years' of work experience. All faculty are required to maintain currency in their field. There are no requirements for professional credentials.
