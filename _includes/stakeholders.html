Target Students

High school graduates seeking applied, career-focused design education with hands-on learning, smaller class sizes, industry partnerships, and pathways to further credentials. Students who value college pedagogy over university theory-heavy approaches, but want degree-level recognition.

Graduate Outcomes

Graduates will be prepared for roles including graphic designer, UX designer, brand strategist, content strategist, design coordinator, creative project manager, freelance designer, and design entrepreneur. They will possess portfolios demonstrating both technical competency and strategic decision-making capacity.

Employment outcomes will emphasize adaptability: graduates can work in agencies, in-house teams, startups, gig economy contexts, or launch their own practices. They will be equipped to evolve with technological change rather than be displaced by it.
